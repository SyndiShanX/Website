Free Perk

Shoot the Head off of every Mannequin around the Map
If done Correctly you will hear an Audio Queue
The next Round will Start with the Announcer Saying "Rip Flesh from Bone" and the Zombies will be Replaced by Mannequins that have a Lot of Health
Once you Kill all of the Mannequins, a Perk Power-Up will Fall from the Sky in front of the Nuketown Sign in the Cul-de-sac

Free Self-Revive

Melee the Head off of every Mannequin around the Map
If done Correctly you will Hear an Audio Queue
Behind the Nuketown Sign in the Cul-de-sac there will be a Male Mannequin, when you look at this Mannequin it won't move but if you Look Away it will Run Towards you
You have to run down to the Generators Area with the Male Mannequin Following you and find a Newly Spawned Headless Female Mannequin
Once the 2 Mannequins are United, if you look away from the Mannequins they will Start Running Towards the Green House Backyard
Follow them Without Looking at them, when they make it to the Shed in the Green House Backyard they will go Inside the Shed
A Free Self-Revive will now be Waiting at the Door to the Shed

Insanity Mode

When the Clock in the Cul-de-sac is on 1 and the Nuketown Sign Population Number is 15 there will be a Purple Orb that will Spawn in the Shed in the Yellow House Backyard
Stay Close to the Orb as it Travels Throughout the Map
(If you go too Far Away from the Orb, it will Disappear and you will have to wait for the Clock and Sign to Line Up Again)
The Orb will end up Traveling to APD Interrogation, where it will go into a TV Upstairs
Break this TV using the Melee of the Bowie Knife which will then cause a Code to come out of the Printer to the Left of the TV
Once you Input the Code you're given into Rushmore's Keypad you have 1 minute for Every Player to go to Rushmore and Interact with him to Start Insanity Mode
You will then be Teleported to Round 200 in the Cul-de-sac where you are Confined to the Layout of the Original Nuketown Zombies Map
Instrumental Versions of Different Easter Egg Songs will Play and Zombies will be Running at Super-Sprinter Speed

Main Quest

Activate the Pack-a-Punch and Progress a Round
Go to and Activate Rushmore in the Operations
Obtain the Galvaknuckles and Kill a Zombie using it near the Static TV somewhere in the Bunker, The TV can be in Beds, Diner, or Lounge
After Activating the TV, it will begin to Read a List of Codes following the format:
(letter, number, number, number, number)
Interact with the Clock in the House that corresponds to the Letter of the Code and Input the Time
You are able to Interact with the Clock to make it move in 15 Minute intervals or Melee it to make it Move Ahead an Hour
If you Fail you can Interact with the TV next Round
House - Letter Table:
A - Yellow House
B - Green House
C - Prisoner Holding
D - Transfusion Facility
E - Operations
F - APD Interrogation
After setting all of the Clocks, head to the House that did not have a Clock Set and Interact with the Clock
It will spin Wildly and give you the Time that you Input into Rushmore
(Example: If The time the Clock gives you is 6:15, This means that you must Input 0615 into Rushmore)
After that, Interact with Rushmore to complete the step
Rushmore will now tell you to find a Red Nova Six Crawler somewhere around the Map
Locate the Nova Six Crawler and make him follow you to the Transfusion Facility
Once Rushmore says that you have Successfully Delivered the Crawler, go to Operations and Interact with him
Head to the Storage Room and Activate A.D.A.M. and bring him to the Bunker Stairs between the Green House Backyard and Diner
Once the A.D.A.M. Unit is close to the door, Marlton will React to him and give you the 115 Canister
Bring the 115 Canister to the Shelf in Transfusion Facility then go Interact with Rushmore
Go to the Power House and get a Jolting Jack to Fire an Electric Blast at the Server Core
Once it does it'll allow you to take the Core out and you have to take it to the Core Slot in Operations before the Timer Expires
Place a Telepad next to the Core in the Power House and another in the Prisoner Holding Building so you can Teleport closer to Operations
If you Fail you are able to grab the Core back at the Power House and try again
Around the Bunker there will be Pictures on the Walls with a Green Mist emitting from them
Use the Brain Rot AAT and get a Brain Rot Zombie to go near the Pictures
If done Correctly the Pictures will be Knocked down Revealing a 4 Digit Code
The Pictures are located in the following places:
  -Green House Upstairs
  -Lounge
  -Beds
Once you have Written down all the Codes you must Input them into Rushmore in Quick Succession After you have done so, Interact with Rushmore
There are now 6 Interactable Power Switches with Lights above them around the Bunker
Activate the Power Switches in the Correct Sequence so that all the Lights above the Switches turn Green
Locations and Correct Switch Position are as followed:
  -Diner: Up
  -Beds: Up
  -Generators: Up
  -Solitary: Down
  -Storage: Down
  -Lounge: Down
Once all the Lights are Green you are then able to Reactivate the Power Switch inside the Power House
Once the Power is Restored, go to Operations and Interact with Rushmore
There will be Newly Spawned Mannequins One of the Mannequins will be Glowing Blue
Have every Player go to the Mannequin and Interact with it to Start a Defense Wave
After Defending for Long Enough the Mannequin will break and you can pick up the Arm of the Mannequin
Repeat this process 2 more times, obtaining another Arm and a Head
Once all 3 Parts are Obtained, bring them to the Broken Mannequin in the Chair at the APD Interrogation
Once the Mannequin has been Revived, Rushmore will tell you to go find an Elemental Orb around the Map
Once you find the Orb, Escort it around the Map and bring it to the Mannequin in APD Interrogation
Head down to the computers in the APD Control area and have all Players Interact with them to start the Boss Fight

Boss Fight

Kill Mannequins around the APD Control area to Fill the Empty Soul Canister
Once full, the door of the APD will open, revealing the Avogadro
Once the Nuke lands, leave the APD Control Area and find the backup Soul Canisters around the Bunker
When you find a Canister have every Player in your Game go in that area and have Someone Interact with the Canister to allow it to be Filled with Souls
While you are Filling the Canisters the Avogadro will Attack You
Once you have Filled it with Enough Souls you will hear a Quote and Electricity will fill that room, Blocking access to it
There is a Canister in the Following Areas:
  -Beds
  -Storage
  -Diner
  -Lounge
Once all 4 Canisters have been Filled, head back to APD Control
Shooting the Avogadro will make Him move in the Direction you are Shooting
You must Shoot him so that he gets in Front of the APD door, Once he is in front he will be Sucked into the APD
After all the Audio Quotes from the Facility Overseer the APD Door will Open and you are able to Obtain the Elemental Shard
After you Obtain the Shard the Cutscene will Play and your Game will End
