Yellow Snowballs

Yellow Snowballs are a 1-hit kill and You can hold 10 at a time
Find 4 Sock Puppets around the Map and Hit them with a Snowball
  -Samantha Puppet - Lagoon - Either on a Rock Facing The Ocean, or at the End of The Broken Boat
  -Eddie Puppet - Lighthouse - Either Outside of the Door on the 3rd Floor, or Inside a Window on the 2nd Floor
  -Margwa Puppet - Lab -  Either on Top of a Vent in the Middle of the Room, or on Top of the Shelf with 115 Cans Behind the Projector
Fluffy Puppet - Boat: Sun Deck - Either on Top of the Zipline Holders, or at the Corner of the Roof of the Power Switch Room
Once You Hit all 4 You must find 3 Blue Campfires around the Map
  -CoTD spawn
  -Parkour Mystery Box Spawn to the Left of Spawn
  -Pack-a-Punch Location to the Right of Spawn
Kill Zombies near them, Once the 3 Fires are Extinguished, Go to the Lab
In the Big Room, the Water Tank with a Piece of Meat inside will be Cracked
Shoot it, and the Meat will fall to the ground
Once you Pick it Up, a Timer will Start
You must Deliver it to the Hermit before it gets Too Warm and Rots
If you Fail, You can Pick it Up again
You have to Go in Water to Cool the Meat Down, this will Reset the Timer
Pick it up, Run to the Zipline to Spawn, Take it in the Water to Cool it Down, then Take the Zipline Up to the Lighthouse and Deliver it
You will be Rewarded with Yellow Snowballs
