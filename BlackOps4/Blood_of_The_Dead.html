Docks

  -Get the Morse Code From The Blinking Lamp in Warden's House
  -Enter it on the Morse Code Machine in the Cell where you Get the Acid Gat Part in Sally Port
  -Kill a Zombie by the Medical Table in the Infirmary
  -Shield Blast the Ghost
  -Kill Zombies near it to make it move
  -Escort it to the Portal at the Docks
 or
  -Brute Force the Morse Code
  -Start with Dit or Dash, if Warden Laughs, It's wrong
  -Trial and Error until Done
  -Kill a Zombie by the Medical Table in the Infirmary
  -Shield Blast the Ghost
  -Kill Zombies near it to make it move
  -Escort it to the Portal at the Docks

Power Room

  -Look through the Shield to see the Ghost in the Power Room
  -Go to Building 64 at the Docks
  -Interact with the Sparking Generator and Play 5 Games of Simon Says
  -When the 5th game ends watch carefully for the 3 Machines where the Lights stay on
(For About 5 seconds)
  -Write down the symbols on the Machines that the Lights stayed lit on
  -Get the Punch Card from the shelf with the Candles on it
  -Take card to room above Spawn, insert into computer in a corner to Light up the 6 Monitors
  -Interact with each monitor to switch between the Two Symbols, Write them down
  -Match the Symbols in the Power Room
  -Wait for the Ghost to move to the Symbol and Shield Blast him, and He will Pull the Lever
  -Do this for all 3 Symbols

Showers

  -A Ghost spawns playing a Banjo
  -Interact with the Ghost to take the Banjo and a Blue Circle will Spawn
  -Stay in the Circle and get Kills
  -Give the Charged Banjo Back to The Ghost

Michigan Avenue

  -Find The Ghost in Cafeteria
  -Escort Him to the Portal without Him Taking Damage​

New Industries

  -Find the Ghost (OG Spawn)

  -Stop Him by Shield Blasting him and Shocking him with the Shield Key until he turns Red

Boss Fight

  -Pick up all of the Red Balls
  -Place them all on the Map in Spawn
  -Go Back to the Warden's House and Interact with the Corpse​ 
  -Get weapons back from the Bag on the Floor and Get a Shield after you exit the Cell
