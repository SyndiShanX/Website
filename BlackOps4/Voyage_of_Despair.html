Pack-A-Punch

  -Cargo Hold
  -Turbine Room
  -Poop Deck

Free Kraken

-Kill a Stoker by shooting it in it's glowing spot If done correctly he will drop a key
-Around the map there will be a chest you are able to interact with and open up with the key, fill this chest
  with souls until it disappears
  -State Rooms
  -Turbine Room
  -Galley
  -Mail Rooms
  -Provisions
-After the Chest Disappears, it will move somewhere else, fill it 3 times, there will be an Item Inside of the
​  Chest 
-The Item indicates where the Kraken will spawn
  -Compass: Boat Deck
  -Telescope: Poop Deck
  -Globe: Mid Deck
  -Binoculars: Forecastle
  -Pliers: Aft Decks

Svalinn Guard

-Go to the Safe in the Mail Room and Melee the Code Breaker with the Shield, It will Fall off, Pick it up
-In the Mail Room there are Pieces of a Skeleton and near them there is a Piece of Paper with a Code 
  -Skull: Code (You must Shoot Bottles out of The Way)
  -Long Bone: Code
  -Hand: Code
  -Foot: Code
-There are 4 Smaller Safes that you can place the Code Breaker on
-Place the Code Breaker on it and see what the 4 Digits are 
-The Code will Correspond to a Code Next to a Piece of the Skeleton in the Mail Room
-Go and pick up the Piece of the Skeleton next to the Corresponding Code and then Place it in the Safe 
-If done Correctly, you are able to Melee the Code Breaker off the Safe once you place the Correct Piece in it
-If you did it Incorrectly the Code Breaker will not come off and you will have to go to the Next Round
-Safe locations:
  -3rd Class Berths
  -1st Class Lounge
  -Bridge
  -Millionaire Suites
-Once all 4 Safes have the Skeleton Pieces in them, go down to the Cargo Room and Interact with this Car, It
​  will Sink into the Floor, and The Car will now be Flying above the Ship
-There are Small Green Vials in Pipes around the Map, If you Shoot them, they will Shoot into the Pipe 
  -Bridge
  -Promenade Starboard Deck
  -3rd Class Berths
  -1st Class Lounge
-The Vial will drop out of Another Pipe somewhere on the Map
-After you Shoot 1 Vial and Pick it up there will be another Vial somewhere else, You are able to get 4 vials 
-Exit pipe locations:
  -Engine Room
  -Mail Rooms
  -Millionaire Suites
-Place the Vial you picked up in a White Slot on one Side of the Boat
-If you Shoot the White Slot after placing the Item, a Firework will Shoot up You must Shoot the Firework into
  the Bottom of the Skeleton's Car
(If you miss the Fireworks will Respawn in the Slot a Few Minutes after being Shot)
-White slot locations:
  -Forecastle
  -Poop Deck
-Once the Firework has been Shot and Stuck under the Car, the Skeleton will be Flying around for a While,
  then He will crash into a Chunk of Ice Floating in the Water 
-After a Few Seconds there will be a Glowing Blue Piece of Ice Floating Towards where you Built the Ballistic
​  Shield Once it Reaches the Shield it Upgrades it into the Svalinn Guard
(The Svalinn Guard has more Health than the Ballistic Shield, and the Gun on it now has the Cryofreeze
​  effect)

Low Gravity

-Shoot Down/Throw a Grenade at the following Parts to Knock them down and Pick them up
  -Mask: Shoot an explosive/Throw an Acid Grenade at the fan
  -Crown: Knock down the Crown from here on the Bridge and then when it lands on another ledge you must
​  knock it down again to pick it up
  -Trident: Shoot an explosive/Throw an Acid Grenade above the Odin Statue
-Once You have all 3 Parts, you can place them on the Body in the Galley 
-Once All 3 Parts are on Him, all of the Zombies will jump around like they're in Low Gravity

Specialist Weapon Fire Sale

-There are Sheets of Music that can be placed on Pianos
-Sheet Locations:
  -Promenade Starboard Deck
  -Promenade Port Deck
  -Dining Hall
-Piano Locations:
  -Lower Grand Staircase
  -Dining Hall
  -Upper Grand Staircase
-After you have placed the Sheets of Music on the Correct Pianos, you can Activate the Pianos by Meleeing it
​  with your Specialist Weapon
-It will play a Tune and Glow Blue, Once all 3 are Blue run to the Mystery Box and Melee it with your Special
  Weapon 
-You will be given a Fire Sale and when you purchase the box it will give you a different Special Weapon

Pack-A-Punch Valves

-Shoot the 4 Middle Pistons in the Engine Room with 6 shots of the Cirein-Cròin Crasher or 3 Shots of any
  other Upgraded Elemental Kraken
-The Pistons will stop moving and there will be Lights Flashing above some of the Sets of 3 Red Valves around
​  the Room
-Take note of which Lights are Flashing, If the Left Light is Flashing interact with the Left-most Valve,etc.
-There can be multiple Lights that need to be Turned at each Set of 3 Valves, so Check All of The Lights 
-Once All of The Valves are in the Correct Positions, interact with the Switch in the Turbine Room 
-If done correctly the Pack-a-Punch will be at every Location for that Round
-The Pack-a-Punch can also be used to Replenish Ammo for 5000 points

Main Quest

-Retrieve the Sentinel Artifact at the Poop Deck
-Activate Pack-A-Punch
-4/6 Clocks will Change their Time
-For the Clocks that Change, one of the Pack-A-Punch Elemental Symbols will Spawn nearby
-Clocks and Symbols:
  -Mail Room:
    -Clock: On the wall on the right side of the door way leading down to the Cargo Hold
    -Symbol: Underneath the stairs leading up to the room with the Vapr-XKG wall buy
  -Bridge:
    -Clock: Right behind the 4 dials there is a room with the steering wheel Right above the steering wheel
      there is a clock
    -Symbol: When looking at the steering wheel/clock, the symbol is underneath a desk diagonally back right
      behind you
  -Upper Grand Staircase:
    -Clock: At the top of the staircase engraved in a wooden carving on the wall
    -Symbol: When looking at the clock, turn to the right up the small steps and turn right again and you will
      see a symbol above a door
  -1st Class Lounge:
    -Clock: In the middle of the lounge there is a fireplace The clock is on the fire place
    -Symbol: When looking at the mystery box in the first class lounge Look to the left and you will see the
​      symbol on the right side of the wall
  -Galley:
    -Clock: In the room with the body hanging on the wall With your back to the body on the wall there is a
      clock on the wall to the left
    -Symbol: When your back is to the wall with the body on it, there is cabinets to the left A symbol is on the
      cabinet
  -3rd Class Berths:
    -Clock: When going down to the turbine when you get to the end of the wooden stairs there is a clock on
​      the wall that you face to the left
    -Symbol: Behind some luggage next to the wooden stairs going up
-On The Bridge in Front of the Captain's Wheel there are 4 Turnable Dials that will have the Elemental
  Symbols on them Correlating to a Clock with the same Symbol nearby
-You must Align the Dials at the Bridge to the Minutes of the Clocks with the Same Element, The dials move
  Left or Right in 5 Minute Intervals 
-The Dials at the Engine Room and on the Poop Deck change the hour of the dials, When properly Aligned
​  with the Clocks you will hear a Noise and no Longer be able to Change the Dials
  -Hour Hand Dial Locations and Corresponding Symbols:
    -Poop Deck (Right)
    -Poop Deck (Left)
    -Engine Room (Right)
    -Engine Room (Left)
-There are 4 Outlets that will be Spewing a different Element
-You must Kill an Elemental Catalyst of the Same Element next to the Outlet
-When you do it correctly there will be a Ritual Symbol on the Ground where you Killed them 
  -Catalyst Outlet locations:
    -State Rooms
    -3rd Class Berths
    -Dining Hall
    -Next to Zeus Statue in 1st Class Lounge
    -Upper Grand Staircase
    -In a window on the Aft Decks
-When all Players Stand on the Ritual Symbol, it will Start to Glow
-All Players must Interact with is to be Teleported into a Trial where you must Defeat all of the Zombies
-When done Correctly your Screen will turn Grey and you will be able to pick up the Sentinel Artifact, Do this
  for all 4 Ritual Circles
-You will be able to pick up the Real Sentinel Artifact 
-You must Complete them in Order:
  -Poison
  -Water
  -Electric
  -Fire
-Shoot 9 Leaking Blue Pipes around the Turbine Room with the Jörmungandr's Fang (Poison Upgrade of the
​  Kraken)
-Once you have Shot them all, the Water will Start Leaking in the Boiler Room and put out all the Fires
-Place the Artifact in the Pack-A-Punch when it's in the Engine Room
(It will teleport to the Engine Room the Next Round if it is not Already There)
-Interact with each Planet Symbol:
  -Sun: Forecastle
  -Mercury: Mail Rooms
  -Venus: Millionaire Suite
  -Moon: Lower Grand Staircase
  -Mars: Boiler Room
  -Saturn: Bridge
  -Jupiter: Engine Room
  -Neptune: Aft Decks
  -Uranus: State Room
-Interact with the Solar System Model in the Cargo Hold, and the Planets will Flash in the Order that you need
  to Shoot Them In
-When you Shoot a Planet a Blue Orb will fall from the Sky and land in the corresponding Planet Symbol
  Location
-You must Interact with the Blue Orb before a Timer runs out, Repeat this for each Planet in the Order until
  you reach the Sun
-The Final Blue Orb will be in the Forecastle at the front of the Ship 
-Once you Interact with it, it will Spawn in Ice Blocks, You must choose a Path and Destroy Ice Blocks with
​  Powerful Weapons on your way to the Poop Deck
-Once you get there you must Destroy the Final Ice Block
(You will know you've Completed it of your Screen Flashes and goes Black and White)
-An Orange Ritual Symbol will Spawn where the Final Ice Block was, which leads to the Boss Fight 
(If you take too long to Destroy the Final Ice Block, your Screen will Freeze and you will need to retry by
​  Interacting with the Orb at the Forecastle)

Eye of Odin Boss Fight

(Recommended Weapons: Hellion Salvo & Kraken (Regular PaP'd or Purity Upgrade))
-You will Start the Fight by being Teleported Underwater, Swim to the Sentinel Artifact and all Players must
  Interact with it
-You will be Teleported back to the Poop Deck
-The Iceberg will Rise out of the Water and then Odin's Eye will Spawn and Teleport around, then lots of
  Zombies, Catalysts, Stokers, and Blightfathers will Spawn
-Once you have Killed all them the Phase will End
-You will be Teleported to the Engine Room Again, then Lots of Zombies, Catalysts, Stokers, and Blightfathers
  will Spawn 
-Once you have Killed all them the Phase will End
-You will be Teleported to the State Rooms, then Stokers and Zombies will Spawn around you
-The Eye will Sometimes Appear in the Doors that exit to the Forecastle and Shoot an Ice Beam down the
  Hallway
(When the Eye is Firing its Laser you are able to Shoot it in the Pupil)
(If you are Caught by the Eye's Beam it will Freeze you and you must Melee)
-When you have done Enough Damage to the Eye, the Phase will End and you will be Teleported out
-It is recommended to Shoot at the Eye with the Upgraded Hellion Salvo in order to kill it faster
-You will be Teleported to is the Promenade Starboard Deck, and The Eye will be off the Edge of the Ship and
  you must Shoot it While it is Firing Its Laser 
-When you have done Enough Damage to the Eye, the Phase will End and you will be Teleported out
-Finally, you will be Teleported back to the Poop Deck, and The Eye of Odin will be Teleporting around, You
  must Shoot It in The Pupil while it is Firing Its Laser 
-When you have done Enough Damage it will go to the back of the Deck and do a Charging up Animation,
  Shoot it with the Regular/Ice Kraken in order to get it to Stop Charging 
-If you fail to stop it, it will down all Players (Unless they are using Dying Wish) 
-Damage it until it Starts the Charging up Animation and Stop it 2 more Times, After the 3rd time of Stopping
​  it from Charging its Ultimate Attack it will be defeated
