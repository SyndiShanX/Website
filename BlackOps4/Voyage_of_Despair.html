Eye of Odin Boss Fight

(Recommended Weapons: Hellion Salvo & Kraken (Regular PaP'd or Purity Upgrade))
You will Start the Fight by being Teleported Underwater, Swim to the Sentinel Artifact and all Players must Interact with it
You will be Teleported back to the Poop Deck
The Iceberg will Rise out of the Water and then Odin's Eye will Spawn and Teleport around, then lots of Zombies, Catalysts, Stokers, and Blightfathers will Spawn
Once you have Killed all them the Phase will End
You will be Teleported to the Engine Room Again, then Lots of Zombies, Catalysts, Stokers, and Blightfathers will Spawn
Once you have Killed all them the Phase will End
You will be Teleported to the State Rooms, then Stokers and Zombies will Spawn around you
The Eye will Sometimes Appear in the Doors that exit to the Forecastle and Shoot an Ice Beam down the Hallway
(When the Eye is Firing its Laser you are able to Shoot it in the Pupil)
(If you are Caught by the Eye's Beam it will Freeze you and you must Melee)
When you have done Enough Damage to the Eye, the Phase will End and you will be Teleported out
It is recommended to Shoot at the Eye with the Upgraded Hellion Salvo in order to kill it faster
You will be Teleported to is the Promenade Starboard Deck, and The Eye will be off the Edge of the Ship and you must Shoot it While it is Firing Its Laser
When you have done Enough Damage to the Eye, the Phase will End and you will be Teleported out
Finally, you will be Teleported back to the Poop Deck, and The Eye of Odin will be Teleporting around, You must Shoot It in The Pupil while it is Firing Its Laser
When you have done Enough Damage it will go to the back of the Deck and do a Charging up Animation, Shoot it with the Regular/Ice Kraken in order to get it to Stop Charging
If you fail to stop it, it will down all Players (Unless they are using Dying Wish)
Damage it until it Starts the Charging up Animation and Stop it 2 more Times, After the 3rd time of Stopping it from Charging its Ultimate Attack it will be defeated
