​Telescope Quest line

  -All Players must shoot The Rod above the Mausoleum using Silver Bullets at the same time
  -If done correctly, the rod will disappear, and a Beam of Light Will Spawn
  -Go to the North Atrium Bridge and you'll see 3 Wheels that Control the Rings over Atlas' Head
  -You have to Line Up
  -The Beam will Destroy his Head and Connect to The Telescope in the Greenhouse Laboratory
  -Turn the Middle wheel until the Green Light is in place
  -Turn the Left wheel until the Blue Light is in place
  -Turn the Right wheel until the Red Light is in place
  -Search for 3 Zodiac Symbols
  -There will be 3 different Scratch Mark Patterns in the Room
(Sometimes a Scratch Mark does not spawn, this will count as a 0)
  -Billiards Room Zodiac: Under the Chess Table
    -Billiards Room Scratch: Left of the Bookcase
    -Billiards Room Scratch: Above the Cabinet Closest to the Smoking Room
    -Billiards Room Scratch: When walking in from the Smoking Room, under the Table to the Right
  -Entrance Hall Zodiac: Under the Table Straight Across the Room on the left of the Perk Machine
    -Entrance Hall Scratch: On the Shield to the Right of the Perk Machine
    -Entrance Hall Scratch: On the Corner to the Right of the Portal
    -Entrance Hall Scratch: When you walk in, it's on the Left above the Painting
  -Trophy Room Zodiac: Back Left of the Room, on the Right side of the Closet where the Lamp is
    -Trophy Room Scratch: To the Left of the Fireplace across from the Zodiac
    -Trophy Room Scratch: On the Desk to the left of the Bed
    -Trophy Room Scratch: Through the Window on the Left after you walk through the Fire Trap
  -Main Hall Zodiac: To the Right of the stairs, before going up to West Spawn
    -Main Hall Scratch: To the Right of the Stairs, before heading to the Sentinel Artifact
    -Main Hall Scratch: To the Right of the Clock, above the Chimney
    -Main Hall Scratch: When walking in from the Forest, directly in the Upper Left Corner of the Room
  -Library Zodiac: In the Broken Cabinet, to the Left of the Gun Cabinet
    -Library Scratch: First Window to the Left of the Zodiac Sign, in the Upper Right Corner
    -Library Scratch: When you're facing the Zodiac sign, turn around and it will be on the Box
    -Library Scratch: Next to the Left Foot of the Dinosaur
  -Wine Cellar Zodiac: Bottom Row of the Wine Rack Closest to the Perk Machine
    -Wine Cellar Scratch: On the Big Barrel after going down the Stairs on the Right
    -Wine Cellar Scratch: Door Cabinet between the Left Stairs and the Perk Machine
    -Wine Cellar Scratch: On a Barrel behind the Buildable Table
  -Dining Room Zodiac: On the Wall between a Broken Pillar and Green Couch
    -Dining Room Scratch: On the Ceiling in Front of the Stairs
    -Dining Room Scratch: Corner of the Dining table that's near the Stairs
    -Dining Room Scratch: When entering from the East Hallway, across the Room On the Wall behind the
      Tables
    -Dining Room Scratch: Ceiling near the Entrance from the Main Hall, next to a Painting
  -Add up the Scratch Marks in Each Room, and Order the Symbols from Least to Greatest
  -Head to the Greenhouse Laboratory and go up the stairs to the giant telescope
  -You'll see a rotating panel with all of the possible zodiac symbols on it
  -Input your three zodiac symbols in order and confirm them by knifing the panel
(If done incorrectly, the three symbols will disappear and change)
  -If done correctly, all three symbols will remain
  -Pull out your shield and hold interact with the dome crank to open a section of the roof
  -When the dome is fully open, melee the crank with the shield and it'll be locked open
  -Activate the electric trap in the Greenhouse Laboratory and have everyone take out their shields and run
​    through it, then run upstairs and Shield Bash the Telescope at the same time
  -If done correctly, a beam will fire into the air and reveal the full moon
  -Pick up the Stone Slab that Spawns under the Telescope
  -All Players must Interact with The Stone Slab
  -You will be locked in the Area and must Survive while Zombies, Vampires and Werewolves Attack You
  -Pick up the Stone Slab to complete The Telescope Quest Line, and You'll be rewarded with a Max Ammo

  -First set
    -Smoking Room
    -Library right of trap
    -Library left of trap
    -Billiards Room

    -Main Hall Left
    -East Gallery
    -Main Hall Right

  -Third set
    -Trophy Room
    -Master Bedroom
    -Music Room
    -Dining Room

  -Interact with The 3 Knights and a Crystal will Spawn
    -Main Hall
    -Greenhouse Terrace

  -After all 3 crystals are in position, 3 Ghost Knights will Spawn
  -Kill Zombies near each Knight to move them towards the Pack-A-Punch, They will Form a Triangle
  -Kill a Werewolf between the Knights
  -Pick up the Stone Slab that Spawns
  -All Players must Interact with The Stone Slab
  -You will be locked in the Area and must Survive while Zombies, Vampires and Werewolves Attack You

Effigy Quest line

  -Go to The Graveyard, certain White Birch Trees will have Leaves falling down from them
  -Shoot down 1 Branch from each tree, Pick up all 5 Branches
  -Find the Gravestone that has 1912 as the Death Date, 1 Player can Interact with it and Enter Spirit Mode
  -Shoot the ground in Front of the Effigy with the Annihilator's Fireball Charged Shot to light it on Fire
  -While on fire, the Player in Spirit Mode has to Interact with the Flaming Effigy
  -They will be pulled onto the Effigy and fall through a Red Corridor, They will enter Afterlife Mode and be
​    Invisible to other players
(But not Zombies)
  -Have the Player in Afterlife Mode go Inside of The Mansion and search for a Ghost
(Usually inside of Perk Machines and Outside of Barriers)
  -Escort the Ghost to the Effigy in the Graveyard until it Spawns a Large Column of Flames
  -Pick up the Stone Slab that Spawns on The Ground
  -All Players must Interact with The Stone Slab
  -You will be locked in the Area and must Survive while Zombies, Vampires and Werewolves Attack You
  -Pick up the Stone Slab to complete The Effigy Quest Line, and You'll be rewarded with a Max Ammo

​Boss Fight

​  -Go to the Stone Door in the Forest and Interact with it to enter the Boss Fight
  -You will fight an Invisible Werewolf Boss, a lot of Zombies, Vampires, Werewolves, and Catalyst Zombies
  -Phase 1
    -A Glowing Green Square will spawn on the ground
    -Rotate The Statues at the Edge of the Arena so that the Beams of Light are pointing at the Green Square 
    -The Beams will turn Green if they're Aligned Correctly
    -Make the Werewolf move into this Green Square by Standing in it, He will become Trapped and Vulnerable
    -A Max Ammo and Carpenter will Spawn
  -Phase 2
    -Survive while Zombies, Catalysts, Vampires, Werewolves, and Crimson Nosferatu's attack you
    -Once You've Survived Long Enough, the Boss Werewolf will jump back into The Arena
    -A Max Ammo and Carpenter will Spawn
  -Phase 3
    -Similar to Phase 1, except the Glowing Green Square is Invisible until found by a Statue
    -Rotate the Statues at the Edge of the Arena until the White Beams turn Green
    -Once all 3 are Oriented correctly, You can Lure the Boss into the Green Square to Damage him
    -Repeat until He Dies
