Free Jotunn Star

Hit the Button to Lower the Wall in the Building at Dry Dock
Wall Jump and grab the Chain Links from the Front of the Boat
Place the Chains in the Wall next to the Door at the Burial Grounds and Repair it



Grab the Chili Chunks Can from the Box behind the Truck on the Storm Bridge
Place the Can on the Table in the Skallen Market
Wait until Round 10+ for the Bear to Spawn on a Frost Round, then kill it and pick up the Lantern



Place the Lantern on the Table in the Burial Grounds Room
Look at the Symbols around the Base of the Table, find them on the Walls and Interact with them in Order (Left, Right, Back, Front)



Once you Interact with the Symbols in Order, Astrid's Ghost will spawn and Guide you across the Map into the Top of the Lighthouse
There will 2 spots where you have to fill her with Souls to continue, once you fill her near the Zipline to the Lighthouse she will spawn a Parkour Path that you have to jump across
Enter the Top of the Lighthouse and pick up the Jotunn Star



Main Quest

Transmitter

Pick up the Crowbar from the Shelf upstairs in the Lighthouse after Obtaining the Jotunn Star



Interact with the Shipping Crates around the Map until you get the Flak Cannon Shell




Place the Flak Shell into the Cannon facing Spawn, the melee it with the Jotunn Star to fire it



Search the Radio Equipment and pick up the Transmitter, the place the Transmitter in Tyr's Head



Watch the 2 lights avove the Control Panel for your 2 Frequency Sets
The Lights will blink a number of times, take note of both sets of 2



Unfreeze the Ascender up to the Pylon in the Core Foundry area using a Molotov
Interact with the Radio Equipment and Input your First Frequency, Kill the Zombies that Spawn, then input your Second Frequency


Pick up the Barrage Controller once the Calibration is complete
Strike the Machine Workshop near Melee Macchiato to open it



Irradiated Fish Uranium Sample

Catch the Irradiated Fish at one of the Fishing Spots, an Irradiated Ravager will spawn, then run away
Wait until next Round then find it 3 times around the Map and kill it, then pick up the Uranium Sample  (It shows up as an HVT on the Map)



Glocke Drop Uranium Sample

Call in a Glocke Drop and Kill the Floating Zombies around it until you get all 3 Green Lights to turn on
It will Drop a bunch of Loot along with a Uranium Sample



Genetic Lab Uranium Sample

Drive an ARC-XD car through the vent in ROOM_NAME, jump across the River, then blow open the Door to the Genetic Lab



Look down the Hallway and see what numbers have a Radiation Symbol under them
Convert the number into the Letter of the Alphabet they correspond to  (1 = A, 2 = B, 3 = C, 4 = D, 5 = E)



Find the 2 Jars with heads in them labelled with your letters, place them on the Machine, then pick up the Solution
You will know you did it right if the Solution is Purple, if it is Blue then you have to pour it on the Carcass and try again





Pour the Solution on the Carcass on the other Side of then Room and Pick up the Necrospike, then Lockpick the Cell Door and pick up the Uranium Sample



Atomkraft Core

Place the 3 Uranium Samples in the Atomkraft Claw Machine in the Machine Workshop you blasted open
Place the Uranium Samples around the Board so the needle stops in the Green Spot, the more containers that touch, the more the needle goes up



Pick up the Atomkraft Core, place it on the Crate to the right of Quick Revive, then activate the Generator in the Shed
Defend the Atomkraft Core until it is charged, you'll have to restart the Generator when it gets interrupted



Take the Charged Atomkraft Core to the Storm Bridge and place it on the Barrel



Sunstone

Tyr will suck it up and shoot a chunk off of Dravakar, pick up the chunk and place it on the Fire Spit in the Bloodheim Hall
Light the fire using the Jotunn Star melee, then activate a Desciple Injection and throw Zombies into the fire until it ends, then kill the HVT that spawns



Pick up the Sunstone near the Fire, then place it on the Pedestal in the Church, then melee the Sunstone with the Jotunn Star



Go outside and take Note of the Symbols the 3 arrows on the Sky Compass are pointing at in order  (The arrows have horizontal lines from 1-3)

Find the Symbol Pyres on Floting rocks around the Map and Light them using a Charged Shot from the Jotunn Star in order



**PREPARE FOR THE BOSS FIGHT**

Interact with the Control Panel in Tyr's Head to start the Boss Fight
Shoot Dravakar's Red Weak Spot whenever they appear
Shoot the Floating Heads with the Charged Shot from the Jotunn Star, then deal damage



Kill Dravakar to Trigger the Cutscene
