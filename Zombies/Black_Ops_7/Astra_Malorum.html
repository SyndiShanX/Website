Main Quest

Obtain the LGM-1, then on Round 10+ follow Oscar while he's Patrolling until he gives the Code in the form of Planets
Convert the Planets into Numbers by using their order from the Sun, then enter the code into the Machine in the Observatory Dome, then pick up the Key

1: Mercury 2: Venus 3: Earth 4: Mars 5: Jupiter 6: Saturn 7: Uranus 8: Neptune

Place his Brain into the Perfusion Machine in the Luminarium, activate and defend it, then take it to Machina Astralis and place it on the Machine



Connect the Perfusion Machine, the take note of the Book titles on the Screen
Rotate the Busts in the Archive of Orbis depending on the number of matching books the Bust has in its area  (Each area has 3 sets of Books on Bookshelves that are on the same wall)



Climb up the Ladder and pick up Neptune, then place it in Machina Astralis

Once you hear a Shimmering sound, shoot the Cymbals outside of the Map from furthest to closest, then the one above the Aegis Terminal to lure the Ascendant Eye





Keep shooting the one above the Aegis Terminal until you can jump up and grab the eye, then place the Eye into the Pyramid

**PREPARE FOR THE BOSS FIGHT**

Interact with the Aegis Terminal to start the Boss Fight


Phase 1

Fill one of the Martian Cannons, then fire it by holding Interact on the Aegis Terminal, repeat this 3 times  (Aether Shroud or some Distraction Equipment Recommended)

Phase 2-3

Shoot the Boss until you Reveal a Blue Weak Spot, then shoot that to deal extra Damage  (It is recommended to stay as far away from the Boss as possible while dodging the ranged attacks)

Phase 4

The Boss will throw Purple Electrified Rocks that spawn damage areas around the Arena that will Insta-kill you if you get too close
Defeat Caltheris to Trigger the Cutscene
