Fog Rolling In Easter Egg

You can only attempt this once per game

Pick up the Vermin Part from the open vent in the Supply Depot and place it on the Tray in the Mainframe Chamber
Look at the Chart in the Window going up to PaP and note the order to mix the ingredients by the Number of Drops coming from the Colored Bottles
Shoot the Bottles around the Tray in Order (The bottles with the Blue Caps are Orange, the White Alcohol Bottle is Green, and the Pill Bottle is Purple)



If you shoot them in the Correct Order, the Part will start Smoking, pick it up and go up to the West Hallways and place the Part into 1 of 3 Microwaves



Once it Cooks for 10 Seconds the Smoke will turn Red, pick it up and take it to one of the Blood Puddles on the Stairs leading to Pack-a-Punch, crouch and interact to place it
Shoot the Large Fan on the Wall with the Wunderwaffe DG-2 to have the Part get Sucked up and Shredded



Obtain a Chopper Gunner and Shoot all of the Red Smoking Chimneys to have the Fog Roll in and spawn Loot Crystals around the map for around a Minute

Marine SP Upgrade Quest

Obtain an Marine SP at Blue Rarity and Place it on the Shelf in the Banquet Hall Entrance
Shoot one of the Outside Deer to see what Symbol shows up, switch your Field Upgrade based on the Symbol



Kill Zombies near the Deer Heads while that Field Upgrade is Active to Upgrade the Marine SP on the Shelf
The First Upgrade will Upgrade the Rarity to Purple
The Second Upgrade will Upgrade the Rarity to Gold
The Third Upgrade will Upgrade the Pack-a-Punch Level by 1  You can Save & Quit in Solo to Repeat this to get all 3 Pack-a-Punch Tiers for Free)

Friendly Zombie Easter Egg

Pick up the 6 Mr. Peeks parts around the Map





Assemble Mr. Peeks in the Motor Court to Spawn a Friendly Vermin that will Kill Zombies and Transform into a Parasite which will Transform into a Doppleghast then Die



Jumpscare

Obtain a Weapon with a Thermal Scope and look for a Ghost in 4 Windows around the Map
Look at the Ghost 4 Times, then interact with the Mirror in the Nursery near PhD Slider to get the Jumpscare and Some Loot





Free Wunderwaffe

Obtain a Ray Gun Mark 2 Variant and Complete the Ritual Associated with it
Go to the Nursery near PhD Slider, lay down and shoot the Laundry under the Bed
Obtain a very Powerful Weapon and Shoot 3 Relics around the Map, Destroy the Debris around them, and Destroy the Relic as fast as Possible
If it returns to the Original Location you weren't fast enough and you can try again Next Round



Once you Destroy all 3 Relics, Shoot the Purple Smoke at the Bottom of the Elevator Shaft and it will Drop the Wunderwaffe



S.A.M. Trap Easter Egg

Interact with the 4 Computers around the Map



Watch the Computers when the Round Changes until you find the one that shows a Red Screen
Once you see the Red Screen quickly Interact with the Computer and take the Floppy Disk from the Computer
Insert the Floppy Disk into the Computer near Pack-a-Punch and Flip the Round to Infect the Computer
Interact with the Computer once it's Infected to activate the S.A.M. Sizzle Trap, you can Activate it again for $2,000

Raygun Mark II

Obtain the Floppy Disk from the Lab Technician that Spawns in the Mainframe Chamber on Round 10
Insert the Floopy Disk into the Computer in the East Foyer, Kill the Vermin, and Find the 4-Letter Word in all Caps on the Printed Document



Go to the Nursery and convert the Word into Numbers by finding your letter in one of the groups and counting the number of letters in that group for each letter

>

Type the Code into the Keypad next to the Quarantine Cell holding the Doppleghast to Unlock it, Kill the Doppleghast and pick up the Severed Arm



Go to the Ray Gun Mark II Locker in the Armory and Interact with the Panel Next to it to Unlock it



Canisters

Shoot the 4 Crystals around the Map with the Raygun Mark II until one Drop an Empty Canister, then Pick it Up





Throw a Kazimir Device near the Window under Double Tap and pick up an Empty Canister  (You can get a free one from the Service Tunnel)



Check which letter you have by the Tube in the Mainframe Chamber and go the the Service Tunnel near the Trapped Doppleghast
Throw Tomahawks at the Center Valve and the Valve matching your letter until they are on Fire
Throw a Tomahawk at the Tube with the Canister inside and pick it up



Explosive Canister - Ray Gun Mark II-W

Place the Canister into the Yellow Machine at Shem's Henge



Interact with the Machine to Spawn an Abomination, have it Beam the 3 Small Rocks then Smash into them



Once all 3 Rocks are Glowing and Floating, the Pad will Start Siphoning from them, protect the Pad from the Vermin that Spawn
Once the Pad has Siphoned enough Energy you can pick up the Explosive Canister from the Machine
Place the Explosive Canister in the Workbench in the Service Tunnel to Upgrade the Ray Gun Mark II



Toxic Canister - Ray Gun Mark II-R

Obtain 4 Seeds by Shooting the Purple Mushrooms around the Map with Napalm Burst or by using Explosives







Place the Canister into the Yellow Machine in the Conservatory



Place a Seed in all 4 Growing Chambers and Protect them from the Zombies that Spawn



Once all 4 Seeds have Grown you can pick up the Toxic Canister from the Machine
Place the Toxic Canister in the Workbench in Spawn to Upgrade the Ray Gun Mark II



Light Canister - Ray Gun Mark II-P

Pick up the 2 Reflectors from Fountains around the Map



Open the Ritual Room using th Essence Bomb

Place the Canister in the Yellow Machine in the Ritual Room



Place the Reflectors on the Stands around the Room and Rotate them using Melee until the Beam hits a Doppleghast Statue



Melee the Crystal to send a Pulse that will Spawn the Doppleghast



Kill 3 Doppleghasts to Charge the Canister, then you can pick up the Light Canister from the Machine
Place the Light Canister in the Workbench in Richtofen's Room to Upgrade the Ray Gun Mark II



Main Quest

Elevator

Repair the Elevator using the Fuse from the Maintenance Worker and the Circuit Board from Richtofen's Server





Once you Repair the Elevator you can call it to Spawn some Zombies, Kill then and Call the Elevator again

Obtain an Upgraded Ray Gun Mark II and Complete 3 Lockdowns around the Map by Interacting with Machines attached to Portaits
Once you Open a Liminal space you can Obtain the Ritual Items for each Character in the Liminal Spaces

Distillery - Ray Gun Mark II-W



Obtain the Service Bell from the Study, you have to break the Glass if it's in a Cabinet





Brain Rot a Zombie and Ring the Bell, the Zombie will go behind the Bar on the Overlook, drink a Flask and Explode, pick up the Flask
Place the Flask on the Altar in the Liminal Distillery and Kill the Mangler to Complete the Ritual



Library - Ray Gun Mark II-R



Pick up the Audio Log, Ledger, and Nuclear Plant ID Badge while in Aether Shroud  (You can get a free one from the Blue Canister in the Mainframe Chamber)



Audio Log



Ledger



Nuclear Plant ID Badge



Once you have all 3 Items, Interact with the Glowing White Books on the Bookshelves in the Library in Order: Right, Left, Center, then pick up the Report from the Room



Place the Report on the Altar in the Liminal Library and Kill the Doppleghast/Amalgam to Complete the Ritual, then pick up the Sentinel Artifact



Banquet Hall - Ray Gun Mark II-P



Obtain the Ritual Elixir from the Liminal Banquet Hall



Interact with 3 Goblets around the Map and Kill the Desciple that Spawns to reveal a Symbol



There are Numbers around the Elevator Shaft in the Banquet Hall that Correspond to the Paintings in the Liminal Banquet Hall





Input them into the Safe in the Room with Death Pereption based on the following Symbol Order, then pick up the Antler from the Safe



Place the Antler on the Altar in the Liminal Banquet Hall and Kill the Desciple to Complete the Ritual, then pick up the Sentinel Artifact
