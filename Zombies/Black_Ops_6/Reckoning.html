Easter Egg Songs

Remember Us

Interact with 3 Bunny Headphones



Samantha's Peace

Activate PaP, then go to the Metal Detectors in Reception and go through them in sequence
Go through the Metal Detector on the Left, Right, then Center; Repeat this sequence 4 times to activate an Easter Egg Song



Samantha's Soul - Free Self Revive

Go to the Director's Office and Shoot the Pool Balls in Order to Spawn a Free Self Revive and activate an Easter Egg Song
Shoot the 1 Ball on the First and Second Shelf, then 5 on the Bottom Shelf to spell out 115



Free Powerups

Shoot Powerup Items around the Map





After Shooting all of the Other Powerups a Fire Sale Powerup will Spawn



Free Perks

Mr. Peeks

Shoot Mr. Peeks on top of the Machine in the middle of the Mutant Lab from the Executive Suite and Interact with Mr. Peeks
Fly through all of the Purple Hoops without missing any to get a Free Random Perk Powerup, you can retry if you miss any

Quantum Vermin Mini-game

Activate Boost in Sublevel 10, and kill as many Quantum Vermin and Zombies as quick as possible to earn Score on the Scoreboard above the Control Panel
You will earn a Free Perk Powerup if you earn over 2000 Points (Quantum Vermin = 200 Points, Zombies = 10 Points)

Garbage Picker

Interact with all 8 Trash Cans around the Map, 4 of which will drop Loot





Once you've interacted with the 4 Trash Cans with Loot, go to Reception and Interact with the Golden Trash Can to get more Loot



Jumpscare

ADS at the Picnic Blanket on the Hill from Executive Suite with a Sniper Rifle to Activate the Jumpscare



Chicken Bucket Hat

Knock the Chicken Bucket down using an Explosive in the Kitchen in the Executive Suite
Pick up 6 Chicken Drumpsticks around the Room





Interact with the Bucket to Equip it

Free Vending Machine Loot

Melee the Vending Machine Door once per Round to dispense a Random Item, or break it using Melee Macchiato to dispense a bunch of Loot

Target Practice

Activate PaP, the Stand on the Project Janus Logo in Reception and Shoot the Symbol on the TV
Shoot all of the Red Targets before the Timer on the TV runs out to get an Ammo Mod, repeat this to get an Aether Tool, then an Aetherium Crystal

Round 1-10: Rare Aether Tool - Raw Aetherium Crystal
Round 11-20: Epic Aether Tool - Refined Aetherium Crystal
Round 21+: Legendary Aether Tool - Flawless Aetherium Crystal



Aetherella

Obtain the Gorgofex, then Spray Paint the Bathroom Stall door in Reception
Shoot the Aetherella Figurine with a Charged Gorgofex Shot to make Aetherella follow you
Go through the Beam in Sublevel 10 to Charge Aetherlla, then go through the Beam again and Bump into her to have her Follow you and Kill Zombies
You can charge her multiple times

Klaus

Pick up Klaus' Legs from one of the following areas in Android Assembly



Shoot 2 Arms off of Kammando Klaus' and Pick them Up (1 per Round)



Place the Parts on Klaus' Body near the Entrance to Android Assembly
Have an Uber Klaus throw a Shock Ball near Klaus to Activate him, then pass the Round for him to finish playing on the Computer
Escort Klaus to a SAM Trial Station, then Activate and Complete it

Artifact Folders

Find 3 Folders containing various Artifacts in the Director's Office and Executive Suite and 1 through the Teleporter in Dark Entity Containment





Sort the Folders by the Archival Date in Chronological Order, then enter that 4 digit code into the Computer through the Teleporter in Dark Entity Containment









CASTER Turret Upgrade

Activate the C.A.S.T.E.R. Turret 5 times then Kill Zombies and pick up the Hard Drive
Place the Hard Drive in the Container in Quantum Computing and defend it, you can repeat this by activating it 10, then 15 Times

DNA Vials

Turn on the Particle Generator, then kill the Geneticist in the Mutant Lab and pick up the Key Card
Open the Cabinet near the Tank containing Richtofen's Family and pick up the DNA Vials



The Gorgofex

Get the Codewords from the Monitors in Mutant Lab



Use the First Letter of each word and find the Element using those letters on the Periodic Table and Input the Element Number of that Element (Start with 0's if it's not 3 Digits)







or

Kill 3 Vermin near the Container with the Cyst to grow it, Kill 3 Zombies near it, then pick it up



Go to 3 Areas in Quantum Computing and fill the Cyst by draining the Energy from 3 Aether Trees



Go to Dark Entity Containment and Interact with a Beeping Computer, then quickly interact with the other 3 around the Room



Break the Uber Klaus' Shoulder Plates and Lead him onto the Electric Panel on the Floor
Kill The Forsaken and pick up the Ball to Obtain the Gorgofex



Gorgofex Blanchard Upgrade (Cannon)

Press the Button in the Control Room in Reception
Protect the Klaus Bot that Spawns, then go to Quantum Computing to reactivate the Elevator

Fly down the Elevator and Interact with the 3 Red Switches on the Walls, then Shoot the Blue Orb at the Top of the Elevator with the Gorgofex
Kill the Electric Zombies with the Gorgofex to Reveal Panos, Interact with Panos to suck him into the Gorgofex and Upgrade it





Gorgofex Panos Upgrade (Non-Cannon)

Grab the Strauss Counter from Quantum Computing and follow it to the Location with a 9+ Reading (There are Multiple Spawns)
Shoot the Object with a Charged Gorgofex Shot and pick up the Life Drain Serum



Pick up the Fume Hood from the Bioweapons Lab, a PC Fan in the Dark Aether Containment Room, and the Wires outside of Quantum Computing


Build the Gas Chamber outside of Blanchard's Door and defend it to Kill Blanchard
Pick up his Life Essence and bring it to Bioweapons Lab and place it in the Fume Hood next to the Cyst to Upgrade the Gorgofex



Aether Blade

Find 3 Paintings around the Map and Place them on the walls in the Director's Office


Use an ARC-XD to go under the Forsaken and Explode near the Aetherium Crystals to Spawn Crystals on the back of the Box containing the Forsaken
Break the Crystals to get the Safe Key



Use the Mr. Peeks Field Upgrade in front of the Painting behind Richtofen's Desk to knock it down and Reveal the Safe
Unlock the Safe using the Key, Kill the Disciple and Zombies that spawn, then pick up the Aether Blade



Main Quest

Move the Mop Bucket in Reception under the Sprinkler by Meleeing it so it can fill up for Later



Open the Box near the Mangler in a Containment Tank in the Mutant Lab and pick up the Syringe



Go into the Particle Accelerator Beam while in Low Gravity, a body will fall onto the ground, then interact with it to fill the Syringe with their Blood
Interact with the Monitor next to the Mangler in a Tank in the Mutant Lab



Kill the Mangler and pick up the Mutant Injector, then scan yourself at the Locked Door in Dark Entity Containment after using the Mutant Injector



Melee the Container with the 3 Green Lights in Quantum Computing using Melee Macchiato to open it
Pick up the Brain from Quantum Computing and take it to the Teleportation Lab and Place it into the same Machine



Boost the Particle Accelerator to spawn Quantum Vermin



Fill up the Soul Box and Kill at least 1 Quantum Vermin using the Gorgofex or Shadow Damage (Dark Flare, LT33 Kazamir, or Shadow Rift)
Once you collect enough Souls you can Interact with the Blue Orb on top of the Soul Box to Charge the Gorgofex
Shoot the Portal in the Teleportation Lab with the Charged Gorgofex to open it



Shoot a Charged Gorgofex Shot at the Room above the CASTER Trap in Quantum Computing and pick up the Fungal Head



Place the Fungal Head in the Mop Bucket you Filled up Earlier, then pick it back up



Kill Zombies while Holding the Head to fill it with Souls while avoiding the Purple Gas Cloud that follows you
Take the Head to the Portal in the Teleportation Lab and Interact with it once you collect enough Souls

Pick up a Vacuum-Seal Device from one of the blue Project Janus crates around the Map





Throw it at one of the Purple Objects in Reception, Quantum Computing, Mutant Lab, or Executive Suite to Capture it
Take the Device to the Pedestal in the Center of the Room in the Teleportation Lab, Repeat this 4 times



A lockdown will start, flip the glowing Switches in the order they light up when you hear the Alarm to activate the Teleporter

**PREPARE FOR THE BOSS FIGHT**

Interact with the Sentinel Artifact and Pick a Side to Start the Elevator to the Boss Fight
Fill 3 Soul Boxes on the First Floor the Elevator stops at
Break all of the Aetherium Crystals on the Second Floor



Kill either S.A.M. (Cannon) or Richtofen (Non-Cannon) depending on who you decided to side with to Trigger the Cutscene
