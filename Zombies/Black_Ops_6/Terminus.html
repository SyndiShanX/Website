Free Beamsmasher

Turn on Power and Lift the Pack-A-Punch
Activate three laptops around the map
  -Outside Stamin-Up Room in the Corner
  -Outside Quick Revive Room in the Corner
  -On the Platform down by the Docks
Obtain Dead Wire on your Gun, ride the elevator that has the PaP machine on it
Shoot the Fuse Box inside the Window around halfway up, Electricity will travel to the next Fuse Box, Keep Shooting them until you get to the Green Door that Opens
Shoot the zombie inside the Room and it will drop a Fob
Go to the Sea Tower and open the Briefcase and retrieve the Multiphasic Resonator
Go back to the Research Lab and put the Resonator into the Computer
3 Sticky Notes will will be Taped to the Computer, that show you which Symbol corresponds to X, Y, and Z
Look on the Whiteboard to get the Values corresponding to the 3 variables
Solve the 3 Algebraic Equations on the Whiteboard, then Input the answers into the Computer
Wait until the Resonator is Calibrated and Pick it up (Should work after you pass a Round after Round 9)
Grab the Resonator from the Computer
Go to 3 different islands and Fill up an Orb with Sparks

  -Go to Castle Rock Island (Right Side of the Map) and Fill the Glowing Blue Orb
  -Head to Crab Island (Large Island at the Bottom of the Map) and Fill the Glowing Green Orb
  -Head to Temple Island (Top Left Corner of the Map) and Fill the Glowing Purple Orb

Repeat the following Steps at Each Island

  -Place the Multiphasic Resonator in the Orb and Zombies will Spawn
  -The Orb will Zap a Zombie, which will make it Glow, Kill them and it will drop a Spark, Pick up the spark take it to the Orb and Place it
  -Once you Collect 2 Sparks Pick up the Resonator and go to the next Island

Pick up the AMP Munition
Go back to the Research Lab, and on the workbench there you can craft the Beamsmasher

Main Quest

Obtain the Beamsmasher
Look for a Tentacle Trap that has a Broken Crate and a Red Keycard under it
There is one in the Juggernog Room and 2 more on the Doors connecting the Lab to the Caves
Activate this trap and shoot the tentacles with your newly acquired Beamsmasher, and a hard drive will fall out
Take the Hard Drive to Peck and Place it into the Item Slot

Locate the Numbers for the Chamber Code

  -The Hour hand on the Clock in the Holding Cells by Quick Revive
  -Playing Card Number on the Bulletin in the Mess Room
  -Days Since the Last Injury in the Engineering Room

In the center of the Lab there will be a Chamber containing Nathan
Input the code, and have all Players turn the Valves on the Chamber at the same time
Nathan will be released, and the Lab will enter Lockdown
Kill Nathan, then dive into Water and find his Key Card
Head under the Shipwreck and enter the Hatch using the Ladder in the Middle
Interact with the Nodes to start a Lockdown in the Room, Survive, then Take a Node Connector to Repair the Pipeline across the Map
Looking for a Missing Section that is Sparking and Place the Connector There

The Missing Section can be in the following Locations:

  -Crab Island, across from the Workbench
  -Under the Sea Tower
  -In the Sea Caves by Speed Cola

Retrieve the Hacking Device from Peck
Go to the 3 Buoys around the Map and Hack them by Holding Interact with the Hacking Device
A 2 Minute timer will start, Hack the Next Buoy before that Expires
Once you Hack the Third Buoy, run to the Lab as a 5 Minute Timer will Start
There are 3 Bombs inside the Lab that you have to Defuse before the Timer Expires
Two are on the Lowest Floor of the Lab, and the Last one is directly above Melee Macchiato

**PREPARE FOR THE Boss Fight**

Enter the Boss Fight by Interacting with the Door by Melee Macchiato

The Boss will chase you with its Tentacles if you stay in the Water for too long
When you get the Boss to below 1/3 health, he starts using a Charged Wiped Attack, hide behind the Crates when you you see a Blue Attack
Once you Kill the Boss the Cutscene will Play
