Free Crate Easter Egg

Turn on the 3 Aether Reactors
Shoot the Bunny in Village until you get an Audio Cue and it flies at you (You have to stand still or it will fail and you will have to wait until next round to reattempt)
It will Teleport you to a Dark Area in the Jungle
Find the Purple Orb with a Bunny inside and Shoot it 3 times
Find the Crate and Open it, and take the Items quickly as you will be Teleported out in a few seconds

Free Perk

Turn on the 3 Aether Reactors
Get to The Data Center Traps Section of the Main Easter Egg
Find Sergei's Head Randomly Spawned around the Outskirts of the Map
Take it to Field Hospital and Place it on the Charging Plate
Fill a Trap with a Zombie, and Place the Filled Trap next to the Head to Charge It
The Head will Float and Tell you the Safe Combination, then Explode
Open the Safe in Colonel’s Office to get the Perk and a Piece of Intel

Main Quest

Turn on the 3 Aether Reactors
Talk to Peck in Mission Control
Talk to Ravenov in the Village, and pick up the ID

Go to 3 Cabinets with Keypads around the map:
  -Equipment Storage  -Colonel’s Office  -Engineering Tunnel

Go to Field Hospital and Insert the Serums into the Centrifuge
Pick up the Agent Delivery System

Place the Truth Serum Disperser on the AC outside Mission Control

Talk to Peck until he Says the 3 Names of the People you need to get the Memories from
Go to Data Center, and Unlock the Trap Cabinet, then Pick up a Trap

Capture 3 Mimics Spawned by Random Groups Items on the Ground when they're under 25% Health
Insert the Filled Trap into the Memory Scanner
Once all 3 correct Memories have been Extracted, Pick up the Floppy Disk

Insert the Floppy Disk into the OPC Computer in Planning Office

Talk with Peck and then Ravenov to get the Code for the Aethermeter Locker in Mission Control
Go to Scorched Defense and Pick up the Shovel in the Bunker

Obtain the Aetherium Crystals:
  -Open Lot

    -Find the Pure Aetherium Crystal by looking at the Aura around the Crystal, the Pure one has a White Aura
  -Jungle Defense Dig Site

    -Survive a Lockdown against Hellhounds and Manglers
  -Between Field Hospital and Data Center

    -The Crystal will Teleport around the Map when you get close until you Shoot it with the Alternate Shot from the RAI K-84, then you can pick it      up while it's frozen
Insert Each Crystal into the 3 Aether Reactors

**PREPARE FOR THE BOSS FIGHT**

Go to the Planning Office, and Interact with the Computer
Find the Satellite and Shoot it Down (It's marked with a Question Mark)
(If you fail, pass a Couple Rounds to let the Computer Cool Down)

You will be Shown a Cutscene, then be put into the Boss Fight
Don't go anywhere near where the Boss Slams, it will 1-Hit you
