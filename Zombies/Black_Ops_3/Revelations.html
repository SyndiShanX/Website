Main Quest

Shoot the Gravestones in Spawn from Left to Right in Order with any Bullet Weapon 2, 3, 1, 4
Build the Keeper Protector
Look for a Triangular Stone at the Back of a Launch Pad on the Ground
Once you Locate the Stone, Summon the Keeper Protector, take it to the Pad with the Stone, use the Pad then use the Opposite Pad and the Ritual should start
Defend the Keeper Protector, if he dies Restart the Ritual
Once the Ritual is complete, a Reel will drop, place it in the Audio Player in Nacht Der Untoten across from the Tesla Control Panel
Go inside of the Apothicon and throw a Lil' Arnie into all 9 Holes with Green Smoke around the Edges
A Wave of Margwas will spawn every 3 Holes you throw a Lil' Arnie into
A reel will drop on the Bridge overlooking PaP, take the reel to the Audio Player in Kino Der Toten across from the Mystery Box
You should hear a Chime if done Correctly or Samantha's Laugh if you Fail

Shoot 6 Sets of Bones using the Upgraded Apothicon Servant (Some Bones must be Shot with a Bullet Weapon first)

  -Spawn go to the Corruption Engine, and Look through the Stones into the Church and Shoot the Wall to Reveal the Bone
  -Origins go to where Speed Cola normally is, Look over the Gap in the Fence, and Shoot the Rock in the Flames to Reveal the Bone
  -Veruckt go to the left of the Corruption Engine and Shoot the Rocks Sticking out until the Bone comes out of the Ground
  -Nacht Der Untoten Shoot the Broken Roof above the Der Eisendrache Portal until the Bone Starts Floating
  -Shangri-La Shoot the Rock above Stamin-Up to Reveal a Skull
  -Der Eisendrache On Shoot the bottom left Corner of a Wall on the Wall Run to Reveal the Bone

Return to Nacht Der Untoten and Shoot the Area just under the Hole in the Roof which will Spawn Sophia's Skeleton, Shoot it and the Final Reel will drop, take it to the Origins Mound
Shoot all 4 Corruption Engine Turrets towards Sophia's Ghost in Nacht Der Untoten by Firing the Laser at the Blue Crystals to the back left/right the Corruption Engines (Some have 2, so Shoot both until you are kicked off the turret and the Laser stays on the Crystal)
Interact with Sophia and Escort Her to Kino Der Toten, She will Power the Teleporter and once all Players are Inside, you will be Teleported to Samantha's Room, grab the Kronorium

Find 4 Apothicon Eggs around the Map, take them to the Fertilisation Chambers in the Acid Pool inside of the Apothicon and Fill them with Souls
The Player that Collects an Egg has to Fill it, Only 1 Egg is on the Map at a Time

  -Spawn close to Quick Revive
  -Spawn on a Barrel to the left of the Launch Pad from Origins
  -Shangri-La laying in one of the Fire Pits
  -Shangri-La at the bottom of the Stairs from the Launch Pad near the Broken Pillar
  -Der Eisendrache at the bottom of one of the Primis Statues
  -Revelations in a bucket in the Pathway between Kino Der Toten and Der Eisendrache
  -Kino Der Toten on a Chair by the Bowie Knife Wallbuy
  -Kino Der Toten on a Viewing Stand above the Staircase to the left of the Stage from Der Eisendrache
  -Verruckt between the 2 Green Zetsubou No Shima vats near the KN-44 Wallbuy
  -Verruckt near the Wunderfizz Machine on the floor
  -Origins inside a box/on the ground opposite the KN-44 Wallbuy
  -Origins near the Stairs by Generator 3
  -Origins on the Pathway behind the Summoning Altar
  -Origins Snow Pile at the top of the Mound
  -Nacht Der Untoten on one of the Fallen Cabinets

Once you fill an Egg, it will Hatch into a Gateworm
Go around the Map with the Gateworm and listen for a Sonar Sound and Vibrating indicating where to place the Gateworm, when you Place it in the Correct Area you will get a Rune
Repeat this process for all 4 Gateworms until you have all 4 Runes

**PREPARE FOR THE BOSS FIGHT**

Go to the Projector Room in Kino Der Toten and all 4 Runes should be on the Ground, and the Puzzle should be Glowing
Have all Players Stand on a Rune and you will be Teleported into the Boss Battle
At the front of the room, the Kronorium will Flip Through Pages and Reveal the Order you need to Interact with the Rune Puzzle
On the other side of the Room, the Runes will Randomly Appear, Interact with them in the Order Shown in the Kronorium
Once the Correct Code is Entered, a Random Challenge Wave will Start
Once the Wave is Complete, Pick up the Summoning Key from the Center of the Room and Throw it at the Following Locations/Items
  -Shangri-La go to the Stairs and Throw it at the Blue Focusing Stone
  -Nacht Der Untoten Throw it at the Red Barrel just outside of the Barrier across from the Spiral Stairs
  -Verruckt Throw it at the Fountain behind the Power Grid
  -Mob of the Dead Throw it at the Poster in the Cellblock
  -Origins Throw it at the Tombstone just outside of the Origins Mound
  -Der Eisendrache Throw it at the Clock above the Pyramid
  -Kino Der Toten Throw it at the Chandelier in the Center of the Room above the Chairs
Continue the the Boss Fight by having all Players go inside the Kino Der Toten Teleporter
Place the Summoning Key on 1 of the 4 Fountains around the Beast mode Area which will begin Charging it
Once Fully Charged, Throw it at Sophia to allow her to Fire a Laser at the Shadowman, Breaking his Shield
Shoot him until he is Inside the Apothicon's Mouth and Interact with the Kronorium to Complete the Boss Battle and Start the Ending Cutscene
