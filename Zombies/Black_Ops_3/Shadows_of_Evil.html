Canal District
  -Obtain the Detectives Badge by Smashing the Box behind a Grate in the Canal, Shocking the Hidden Fuse Box, then grabbing it in Human Form

Footlight District

Waterfront District

Apothicon Sword

Activate the Pack-a-Punch
Get all 3 Symbols by Riding the Tram

Symbols Tool

  -On the Left side right after Leaving Canals
  -On the Right side after Leaving Waterfront
  -On the Left Side after Leaving Footlight
Once you have all 3 Symbols, go to the Rift and enter Beast Mode
Shock the 3 Symbols you have on the Archway (If you fail, try again next Round)
Pick up one of the Eggs
You have to charge your egg at all 4 Statues around the Map, Reveal each Statue by Smashing it Open in Beast Mode
  -Rift
  -Canals
  -Footlight
  -Waterfront
Once You Charge Your Egg at all 4 Statues, go back to the Archway where you got your egg and Pick Up the Sword
Go to the Ritual Room for your Character and Interact with the Keeper to Start Your Upgrade Quest
Nero - Nero's Landing
Jessica - Burlesque
Jack - Ruby Rabbit
Floyd - Boxing Gym
Once you Have the Arch Ovum, go to each Corrupted Ovum Ring and Activate it (1 Per Round)
  -Spawn District
  -Canals District
  -Waterfront District
  -Footlight District
You must Kill the Margwas that Spawn without going down to Complete that Ring
Go back to your Specific Ritual Room and Interact with the Keeper to Upgrade your Sword

Main Quest

Activate the Pack-a-Punch
Obtain the Upgraded Apothicon Sword
Go to Nero's Landing and Interact with the Book on the Ground, it should Start Floating
Go to the Rift and Grab the Flag
Place the Flag at one of the Sparking Areas in each District (2 per District)
Protect the Flag from the Parasites/Meatballs that try to attack it
Keep an eye out for the Shadow Man, he will Spawn around the Flag and Start Charging the Summoning Key
Once you Survive Long Enough without the Flag Breaking, Pick it Up and place it at another Sparking Area
Once you have Charged it a 2 Sparking Areas bring it to one of the Ritual Tables, and Place it Down
Repeat until you have placed a Flag at each Ritual Table

**PREPARE FOR THE BOSS FIGHT**

Go to the Grand Ritual Room and Interact with Each Keeper to start the Boss Fight
Activate all 4 Chalices to Deactivate the Shadowman's Bubble
Once his Bubble is Gone, Shoot hu=im until he is above the Ritual Table, then Activate the Summoning Key to Trap Him
If you Fail, He will spawn another Margwa and Go Back into his Bubble in the Center
Once you Trap him, you Have Completed the 1-3 Player Part of the Easter Egg, if you have 4 Players you can Continue

**4 Player Only**

Have all Player Go into Beast Mode, it will be Infinite Unless you Take Damage from Margwas or the Purple Symbols on the Ground
You have to Cleanse every once in a While by Going Through the Glowing Orbs, if you don't you will go Down
1 Player Has to Call the Tram to any Station
3 Players must Shock the Panels on the Ceiling of the 3 Tram Stations to Charge the Rails, then Run to the Spawn District and Shock the 3 Keepers
If you fail to do this in Time, Try Again
(If anyone Bleeds out during this Step, Calling the Train at any Station will Respawn them)
If you Complete the Step in Time, the Ending Cutscene will Play
