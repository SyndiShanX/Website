How to use Custom Resource Packs on Console (PC/Mobile Required)

You can use any Pack from the Store, but this Tutorial uses Texture Update Beta

Download Texture Update Beta from the Store on PC/Mobile and Console
Delete the Texture Update Beta from the Global Resources Screen on PC/Mobile and Console
(Apply the Pack, click the Green Checkmark, then click the Delete Button)
Once deleted, completely close Minecraft
Move/Import Dokucraft into the resource_packs folder on PC/Mobile
Replace the manifest.json with the one above on PC/Mobile

PC:
"%localappdata%\Packages\Microsoft.MinecraftUWP_8wekyb3d8bbwe\LocalState\games\com.mojang\resource_packs"

IOS:
"Apps/com.mojang.minecraftpe/Documents/games/com.mojang/resource_packs"

Android:
"/sdcard/games/com.mojang/resource_packs"


Launch Minecraft and apply the Texture Update Beta Pack to any World
Join that World on Console and Download the Pack
Apply the Pack to any World/on the Global Resources Section to use it

**DO NOT REDOWNLOAD THE PACK FROM THE MARKETPLACE**
