How to use Custom Global Resource Packs on Xbox

You can use any Pack from the Store, but this pack uses Texture Update Beta

Download Texture Update Beta from the Store
Delete the Texture Update Beta from the Global Resources Screen
Apply the Pack, click the Green Checkmark, then click the Delete Button
Once deleted, completely close Minecraft
Move the Pack into the resource_packs folder using My Files Explorer
Guide:

Launch Minecraft and apply the Texture Update Beta Pack
You may have to reapply it every reload

DO NOT REDOWNLOAD THE PACK FROM THE MARKETPLACE
